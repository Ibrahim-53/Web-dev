OUR ACTIVITIES

CHICKEN REARING

We provide chicks to potential chicken farmers and support them with chick starter feeds to ensure that the chicks grow healthy. After the chicks grow fully, we provide market for the farmers for both meat and eggs. The aim is to ensure that we sustain our population with our own local chicken products without depending on imports.

SEMINARS AND WORKSHOPS

We provide our population with mind lectures and financial literacy skills through our workshops so as to enable them to become financially independent without depending on the government. We educate people and empower them on how to change LIABILITIES into ASSETS

FERTILISER AND MACHINERY PROVISION

We equip our farmers with necessary fertiliser to maximise produce which will result to food security in the coumtry. Also, provision of machinery for harvesting and product storage in order to minimize and elimiminate post-harvest losses.

INSTALLATION OF SOLAR

Investing in renewable sources of energy as an alternative to eliminate losses experienced as a result of power outages and blackouts.

INSTALLATION OF WATER PUMPS

Providing irrigation facilities for our farmers especcilay in drought prone areas to ensure that farming activities go on through out the year despite the season. This will help the country achieve food security and sustain our population within our means.

PRODUCTIVE TREE PLANTING

We provide our farmers with fruits tree seedlings such as Avocado, Mango among others and help them to get market. We also motivate our farmers to plant medicinal trees in order to promote the alternative healthcare systems that exist in our society.

SUNFLOWER CULTIVATION

We help our farmers carry out sunflower cultivation especially in the areas that don't receive much rainfall so as to provide an alternative source of oil which is more healthier. Also, for use for the production of animal feeds especially cows.

MAIZE CULTIVATION

Our aim is to boost the maize cultivation sector in the country since this is a crucial and important sector as far as food security in the country is concerned. We aim to give the necessary and maximum support to these farmers to ensure that we deal with the post-harvest losses especially due to pests such as weavils.

URBAN POVERTY

Poverty is the state of living below 1 dollar per day. Poverty can either be crucial or relative. This initiative targets the CRUCIAL POVERTY This forum aims at giving those people living in towns in poverty opportunities to go back to their rural areas and start participating in the productive economic activities found in the OUR ACTIVITIES section of this platorm.

RURAL - URBAN MIGRATION

This initiative aims at curbing Rural-Urban migration by providing and taking opportunities to the rural ares. This will help to solve the many problems that exist in Urban areas such as Congestion, Unemployment and Crimes.

FINANCIAL INDEPENDENCE

This forum aims at achieveing financial LIBERATION in the society. It aims to reduce the number of dependencies that are there currently. Through the SEMINARS people will be able to find different ways in which they can be able to transorm their LIBILITIES to ASSETS themselves without needing help or intervention by the government.

UNEMPLOYMENT

This Forum aims at reducing the unemployment ratios by empowering the population to use the available resources. The seminars will be able to mae the people understand that education is not meant to prepare them to be employed but it is meant to challemge them to come up with more opportunities in their society. It is meant to liberate them from foolishness and make them wise. To show them that they are not slaves subjected to anyone but individual human beings with the capacity and potential to transform,change and control their existance by themself.

EQUAL LIVING OPPORTUNITIES

The main goal of this forum is to achieve equal living opportunity without any form of discrimination. It aims to enable every human to live a descent if not a comfortable life despite of the status, race, religion or tribe by creating a conducive environment for empowerment, production and motivation.

VALUE ADDITION

The aim of this Forum is to promote value addition through putting idle land into use through empowering people and providing them with the necessary needed resources. This aims to transform and change the Agricultural face in Kenya and proote food security.

ECONOMIC GROWTH

This Forum aims at promoting Economic Growth through production. This is achieved through creating more productive opportunities for the population. This is because, there will be circulation of the money in the economy and taxes for development will be sufficient.

OUR STORY

ECONOMIC EMPOWERMENT FORUM is an initiative that targets the poor and the less prieviledged groups or individuals in the society but have the potential and ready to change their lives and the situation currently existing. The main aim is to attain development in the community. As it is well known, there are four approaches or strategies to the challenge of development. Those approaches include: Economic Approach, Basic Needs Approach, Capabilities Approach and Human Development Approach.

Economic Approach faced many challenges since despite economic growth, many people became poor and it also resulted to the development of slums and informal settlemnts.
Basic Needs Approach on the other hand focused on the basic needs of people where the government used to provide but Amartya Sen came up with the capabilities approach which extends from provision basic needs. Sen argued that humans desire to live full and productive lives.
Human Development Approach on the hand encompasses enlarging people's choices to develop to their full potential and lead a productive and fulfilling life as well as functioning members of their families and communities.

ECONOMIC EMPOWERMENT FORUM is an initiative that focuses on HUMAN DEVELOPMENT APPROACH.The purpose of this program is to create opportunities for the poor and marginalised people in the society.
Our aim is to have an active population that is responsible working towards economic growth and development. We aim at minimizing the poverty levels in the Kenyan society through partnership with various departments.
Our vision is based on the UBUNTU SPIRIT That I am because you Are. I exist because you exist. Our purpose is to foster interpersonall relationship and living for others. In Peace, Love and Unity.
