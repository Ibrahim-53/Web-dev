OUR CATEGORIES

THE ELDERLY

We provide assistance and support in terms of security and market to all those elderly actively participating in farming activities and rearing of livestock.

THE ABLED DIFFERENTLY

We empower the Abled Differently in order to enable them achieve their goals and their desires as far as their economic activities is concerned.

THE UNEMPLOYED YOUTHS

We equip our youths and support them to carry out their economic activities especially agriculture. We take them through the mind lectures and marketing skills so that they can develop their leadershio skills for interpersonal relations and looking for market for their products

THE WOMEN

We empower the women especially the single mums so that they can engage in self employment activities and aslo agricultural activities so that they can be able to take care of their families without bwcoming a burden in the society.
